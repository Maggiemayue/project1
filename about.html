Meet the Band

Kimson

Kimson (she/her) began her musical journey at the tender age of six, discovering a passion for the piano that later expanded to encompass a wide range of instruments. As a graduate of the prestigious Julliard School of Music, Kimson's exceptional talent has graced stages across the nation, captivating audiences with her soulful renditions and virtuosic technique. Beyond her instrumental prowess, Kimson's voice has been described as a blend of warmth, power, and ethereal beauty, often drawing comparisons to legendary vocalists of yesteryears. Offstage, Kimson is a fervent advocate for music education and frequently conducts workshops for aspiring musicians. As a cornerstone of "Harmony of Hearts," her dedication, talent, and spirit continue to be an inspiration to her bandmates and fans alike.

Sigmund

Sigmund (she/her) is a powerhouse of rhythm and creativity. Growing up in the vibrant city of New Orleans, she was immersed in a world of jazz, blues, and soul from a young age. This rich musical tapestry inspired her to pick up the drums at age nine, and she's never looked back since. A graduate of Berklee College of Music, Sigmund is known for her dynamic percussion skills, effortlessly blending traditional beats with contemporary flair. But her talents don't end there. Sigmund is also a gifted songwriter, lending her poetic touch to many of "Harmony of Hearts'" hit songs. Her charismatic stage presence, combined with her unmatched rhythmic prowess, makes her an indispensable part of the band. Off the stage, Sigmund is a passionate advocate for mental health awareness, using her platform to inspire and support others.

Dillon

Dillon (she/her) has always danced to the beat of her own guitar strings. Hailing from the sun-kissed coasts of California, she found solace in the soothing rhythms of the ocean and the melodies of classic rock echoing from beachside bonfires. By the age of twelve, Dillon had already mastered the guitar, drawing inspiration from legends like Hendrix and Clapton. Studying at the California Institute of the Arts, she honed her skills, blending traditional rock with a touch of modern indie. As the lead guitarist of "Harmony of Hearts," Dillon's riveting solos and intricate riffs add depth and dimension to the band's sound. Offstage, she's an advocate for environmental causes, often rganizing beach clean-ups and promoting sustainable living. Her passion for music is only rivaled by her commitment to leaving the world better than she found it.
